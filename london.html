Top Sites To Visit

British Museum

The British museum has one of the largest permanent collection in the world, numbering some 8 million works and best of all, it’s free admission!.

Salisbury Cathedral

Salisbury Cathedral is a prime example of Gothic architecture. Construction started in 1220 and was finished in only 38 years. Its spire height is over 400 feet tall, which was a marvel of engineering for its time. It also has the best surviving of the four original copies of Magna Carta.

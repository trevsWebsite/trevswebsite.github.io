Top Sites To Visit

Louvre

You don't have to be Art enthusiast to enjoy the Louvre, but you may need lots of energy if you plan to see all 652,300 square feet of this largest museum of the world. The louvre contains over 35,000 objects for prehistory to the 21st century.


Palace of Versailles

The Palace of Versailles is one of the most luxurious and lavish palaces in the world. Considered a symbol of absolute monarchy, it may have been one of the reasons for the French Revolution. Just 25 minutes outside of the Paris, the palace and the beautifully manicured gardens are a must see!
